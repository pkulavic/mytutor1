Become a Tutor

Interested in becoming a tutor? Tutors at MyTutor benefit from the reality that they never have to worry about finding clients; we do it for them! In addition, you can set your own hours and take advantage of our network of students. To apply, follow the steps below:


  1. Send an email to apply@mytutormarin.org with the following information:
    • Your most recent high school transcript
    • If available, your standardized test scores (PSAT, SAT, ACT)
    • A brief bio, and a note stating why you would make a good tutor. Be sure to include:
      • Name
      • High School
      • Grade
      • Where you live
      • Your srongest academic subjects
      • Any other relevant information
  2. We will set up an online interview after reviewing your application.
  3. That's all! After your interview, our clients will be able to book appointments with you whenever you are available.
